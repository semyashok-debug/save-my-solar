Case Study: Marco's Solar Battery Solution

[Client]: Marco
[Product Installed]: Alpha Smile G3-S5 with 30kWh Storage

Background

Marco was looking for the best solution for solar battery storage. With rising electricity costs and the need for reliable energy independence, he wanted a system that was both cost-effective and high-performing.

Consultation Process

Marco reached out to Save My Solar for advice. We provided him with an obligation-free consultation, where our team visited his home and:

  • Inspected his existing solar system to check compatibility.
  • Reviewed his switchboard and electrical setup to ensure safety and compliance.
  • Discussed his energy usage patterns and budget to recommend the most suitable solution.

This entire assessment was carried out free of charge, ensuring Marco could make an informed decision without any upfront costs.

Outcome

After considering all options, Marco chose the Alpha Smile G3-S5. He was confident it was the best value solution that met both his energy needs and financial expectations.

Marco can now:

  • Store excess solar energy during the day.
  • Reduce his reliance on the grid and lower energy bills.
  • Enjoy backup power during blackouts, giving him peace of mind.

Case Study: George’s Solar Battery Solution

[Client]: George
[Product Installed]: Alpha Smile G3-S5 with 30kWh Storage

Background

George wanted a reliable and affordable solar battery system to get the most out of his existing solar panels. He was particularly interested in finding a product that would lower his energy bills while also providing backup power for his household.

Consultation Process

George contacted Save My Solar to explore his options. Our team provided an obligation-free home visit, during which we:

  • Inspected his solar panels and inverter for compatibility.
  • Checked his switchboard and household wiring to ensure everything was up to standard.
  • Discussed his household energy habits and future needs to shape a tailored recommendation.

This thorough assessment came at no cost to George, allowing him to understand his options without financial pressure.

Outcome

After reviewing the options, George chose the Alpha Smile G3-S5. He felt it offered the best overall value while giving him confidence that his household energy needs would be met well into the future.

Now, George enjoys:

  • Greater independence from the grid.
  • Reduced power bills through smart solar energy storage.
  • Peace of mind knowing his home has reliable backup power.
