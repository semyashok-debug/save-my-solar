WHAT WE DO

At Save My Solar, we provide end-to-end solar and battery storage solutions designed to help Australians take control of their energy. With over 7 years of experience in the renewable energy sector, we’ve built a reputation for delivering reliable systems and excellent customer service. We start by understanding your energy needs and goals, then design a tailored solar and battery solution that maximizes efficiency and savings. Our team manages everything — from consultation, system design, approvals, and installation, through to after-sales support and monitoring.

Whether it’s reducing your electricity bills, protecting against rising energy costs, or ensuring you have backup power during outages, we make the process simple, transparent, and stress-free. By partnering with trusted manufacturers, we provide high-quality solar panels, inverters, and battery storage systems that deliver performance you can rely on for years to come. At Save My Solar, we don’t just install systems — we help you achieve true energy independence and make a positive impact on the environment!

OUR VISION

Our vision is to lead the transition to a cleaner, greener Australia powered by renewable energy. We believe every home and business should have the opportunity to harness the power of the sun, reduce reliance on fossil fuels, and enjoy the benefits of sustainable living. We see a future where solar energy is not just an alternative, but the primary source of energy for Australians — affordable, reliable, and accessible to all. By continuously embracing new technologies and best practices, we strive to set the standard for innovation, efficiency, and environmental responsibility in the solar industry.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make renewable energy simple, affordable, and accessible for everyone. We are dedicated to providing high-quality solar and battery solutions that deliver measurable savings and long-term value for our customers. Through honest advice, transparent pricing, and professional service, we aim to build trust and lasting relationships with every client. Our team is committed to helping families and businesses achieve greater energy independence while contributing to a cleaner, more sustainable future. We believe that every installation is more than just a project — it’s a step towards reducing carbon emissions and creating a better world for the next generation.
